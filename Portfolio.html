01 / About A designer who actually builds with AI — every day, not just in pitch decks.

I operate at the frontier of AI — combining deep design craft with a hands-on AI toolstack that most designers are still discovering. I use Claude Code, Codex and Gemini to prototype and reason through complex systems at speed; Higgsfield and Vibe Coding for rapid motion concepts; and AI agents via n8n to eliminate manual bottlenecks in the design workflow itself.

With a background in front-end development and motion design, I specialise in turning AI-driven financial dashboards, automation interfaces and complex data products into experiences that feel intelligent, effortless and human. I don't just design for AI — I build with it, every day.
